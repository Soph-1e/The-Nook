“Deadly Cures” was my final project for my 2025 Core Studio Class with Aya Khalifea. The project required us to find a book at the school library and rework it into a different direction only using structure and color. All words had to be derived straight from the book chosen.
The book I chose was called “The Encyclopedia of Medicinal Plants” by Andrew Chevallier. I reworked the book to only include medicinal plants that were also deadly poisons. The book is 24 spreads or 12 pages and includes such plants as Deadly Nightshade and Common Foxglove.

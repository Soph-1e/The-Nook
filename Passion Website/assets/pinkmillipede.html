The Pink Dragon Millipede


The pink dragon Millipede (desmoxytes purpurosea) makes a difficult, but fulfilling pet! Named for their striking pink color, the millipede can be found in the limestone caverns of the Uthai Thani Province of Thailand.

Fun Facts About the Pink Dragon Millipede:

  • Glands that produce hydrogen cyanide and smell like almonds
  • They can fire the hydrogen cyanide at predators
  • They were discovered in 2007

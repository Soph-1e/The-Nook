The Orchid Mantis

The Orchid Mantis is a Southeast Asian bug know for its amazing camouflague. Closly related to the Praying Mantise, these beautiful bugs are steadily growing in popularity as pets!

Fun Facts About the Orchid Mantis:

  • They make a swaying motion to mimic flowers blowing in the wind
  • The females are larger than the males
  • They can change colors from pink to brown to help blend into their enviornment.

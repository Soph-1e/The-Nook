The Rosy Maple Moth

The Rosy Maple Moth is a fluffy, brighly colored moth native to North America (even here in VA!). They come in many colors, but are well known for their bright pink and highlighter yellow variant.

Fun Facts About the Rosy Maple Moth:

  • Adult Rosy Maple Moths don't have mouths or digestive systems. They rely only on food eaten when they were caterpillars.
  • They only live for about a week to mate.
  • They are the smallest silk moths with a wingspan of just under 2 inches

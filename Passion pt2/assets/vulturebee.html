The Vulture Bee

The Vulture Bee (also known as the Carrion Bee) is a stingless bee native to South America and Mexico that exclusively eats rotting meat.

Fun Facts About the Vulture Bee:

  • They produce a honey called "meat honey" that is edible, but not recommended to eat as they don't make more than necessary for their hive unlike honey bees
  • They have extra teeth to tear flesh
  • They are foragers

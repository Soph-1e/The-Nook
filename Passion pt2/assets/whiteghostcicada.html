The White Ghost Cicada

The White Ghost Cicada is a rare insect found in Southeast Asia. They emerge from the ground durring the rainy season and have an extreamly loud mating call.

Fun Facts About the White Ghost Cicada:

  • The appearande of the Ghost Cicada is seen as a harbinger of the rainy season in Thailand
  • The milky appearance of the cicada is camouflague! It helps them blend into the foliage

The Blue Carpenter Bee

The Blue Carpenter Bee is a solitary insect know for its stunning blue coat. Blue Carpenter Bess are native to Southeast Asia, India, and Southern China, and live alone in tree side burrows.

Fun Facts About the Pink Dragon Millipede:

  • They produce a honey that is super thick, with a similar consistency to peanut butter.
  • They create a food called "bee bread" that is a mixture of honey and pollen and is created to be left behind in nests for their young.
